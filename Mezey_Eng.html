Security

Mezey-Cars places great emphasis on ensuring that our cargos are safe to arrive.



CMR insurance -  The CMR insurance protects the cargo during the delivery from the receiption of the goods to the unloading. 

Commodity insurance - commodity insurance provides protection for the entire or partial damage, damage or loss of the stock.

Drivers - Selecting our drivers, we focus on experience and reliability. Through continuous training and education, we ensure that our employees are well prepared to complete orders in all situations.

About

Mezey-Cars entered the freight market in 2014. With our trustworthiness and expertise we have been the supplier of the largest chains of stores, we have been working with the largest companies in Hungary ever since.

We specialize in the delivery of foodstuffs and frozen goods specially designed for this purpose with our lift-mounted refrigerated trucks.

We believe that accurate, reliable service is the supreme value that we can provide to our partners.
